Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I generate images?

Unable to generate images because it violates Gemini's image generation policies. These policies make everyone safer and prevent malicious photo interference. Please ensure your descriptions comply with Google's usage policies.

Is it safe to use? Completely private?

Completely safe and completely private. The app is completely private with no data retention. All photo processing happens entirely on your local device, and we cannot access any of your photos or personal data.

Is it free?

Currently, Gemini has free credits available. You only pay Google directly for AI model usage, typically $0.01-0.05 per processing.

How to apply for Gemini API key?

Go to aistudio.google.com/apikey, sign in with your Google account, apply for an API key, and set up credit card payment. The key points are: Google account login, apply for API key, credit card payment.

What image formats are supported?

Supports common formats including JPEG, PNG, HEIC. The app automatically optimizes image size and quality to ensure the best AI processing results.
