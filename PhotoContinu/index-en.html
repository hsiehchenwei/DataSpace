Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to provide my own API key?

This ensures complete transparency and privacy protection. You directly control all AI processing costs and data flow. We cannot access any of your data or photos.

Will my photos be stored in the cloud?

Absolutely not. All photo processing happens entirely on your local device. We cannot access any of your photos. Your privacy and data security are our highest priority.

How are usage costs calculated?

The app is completely free. You only pay Google directly for AI model usage, typically $0.01-0.05 per processing - very economical and affordable.

What image formats are supported?

Supports common formats including JPEG, PNG, HEIC. The app automatically optimizes image size and quality to ensure the best AI processing results.
